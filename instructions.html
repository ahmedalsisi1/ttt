Instructions

Users

  1. Create a wallet: wallet.tikkox.com
  2. Explore blocks and transactions: explorer.tikkox.com
  3. Keep your seed phrase secured and offline.

Developers

  1. Read the docs: tikkox.org/docs
  2. Use the API/RPC endpoints listed in the docs.
  3. Test on the faucet-enabled testnet (details in docs).
  4. Submit issues or PRs on GitHub.
